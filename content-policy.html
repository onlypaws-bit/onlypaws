Content Policy

OnlyPaws is a platform dedicated to respectful, wholesome, pet-related content. This policy outlines what is allowed on the platform and helps keep the community safe, transparent, and enjoyable for everyone.

1. General principles

Content published on OnlyPaws must prioritize animal well-being, safety, and responsible ownership. Creators are expected to act in good faith and share content that reflects genuine care for their pets.

2. Originality and ownership

Creators must publish only content they own or have the rights to use. Fake, misleading, or stolen content is not permitted.

  • Re-posting content from other creators without permission is not allowed.
  • Misleading representation of pets or situations is not allowed.
  • Accounts may be suspended or content removed in case of violations.

3. Allowed content

OnlyPaws supports a wide range of pet-related content, including but not limited to:

  • Photos and videos of pets
  • Daily routines, playtime, and training moments
  • Grooming and care-related content
  • Educational or informative pet content
  • Creator updates related to pet life

4. Prohibited content

The following content is strictly prohibited on OnlyPaws:

  • Animal abuse, harm, neglect, or exploitation
  • Animal fighting or encouragement of violence
  • Graphic injuries or distressing imagery
  • Illegal activities or promotion of illegal behavior
  • Hate, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Unsafe or dangerous behavior involving animals
  • Sexualized, suggestive, or fetish-oriented content involving animals

5. Enforcement

OnlyPaws reserves the right to remove content or suspend accounts that violate this policy. Enforcement decisions are made to protect the community and the platform.

6. Reporting concerns

If you encounter content that may violate this policy, please contact us at onlypawssupp@gmail.com.
